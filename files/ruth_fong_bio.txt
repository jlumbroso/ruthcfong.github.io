Ruth Fong is a teaching faculty member in the Department of Computer Science at Princeton University, where she teaches introductory computer science courses and conducts research in computer vision and machine learning with a focus on explaining AI models. She received a PhD in Engineering Science from the University of Oxford in 2020, where she was advised by Andrea Vedaldi. She also received a Masters in Neuroscience from the University of Oxford in 2016 and completed a Bachelors in Computer Science at Harvard University in 2015. Ruth received a Rhodes Scholarship in 2015 and an Open Philanthropy AI Fellowship in 2018.
